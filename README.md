## 📌 Project Overview
End-to-end sales analysis of a scale models and toys company across 
**FY2003–2005**, built on an **8-table relational data model** 
(customers, employees, offices, orders, order details, payments, 
products, raw data) connected via Power Query and Power Pivot.

## 💡 Business Insights
- **Classic Cars + Vintage Cars = 58.8% of revenue** — concentrated product mix
- **Motorcycles has the highest margin (41.8%) but only 11.7% revenue share** — underused opportunity
- **Q4 generates 40–54% of annual revenue every year** — strongly seasonal business
- **Top 2 customers = 14.7% of total revenue** — concentration risk
- **$750K outstanding on $9.60M billed** — accounts receivable gap requiring action
- **Gerard Hernandez leads the team**, well above the $929K average benchmark

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques
- **Excel** — Data cleaning, multi-table joins, dashboard design
- **Power Query** — 8-table data transformation pipeline
- **Power Pivot** — Relational data model across all 8 tables
- **DAX Measures** — Margin %, Team Average, custom KPI calculations
- **Slicers** — Year, Product Line, Country, Order Status, Sales Rep
- **Diverging Bar Chart** — Best vs Worst performing products
- **Treemap & Lollipop Charts** — Customer concentration, rep performance
- **PowerPoint** — 10-slide executive presentation
